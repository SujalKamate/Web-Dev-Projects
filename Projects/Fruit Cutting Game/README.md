# 🍉 Fruit Cutting Game

Slice falling fruits with your mouse or touch while avoiding bombs. Score points, progress difficulty, and enjoy arcade-style gameplay.

## Features
- Fruits spawn with gravity and velocity.
- Slice fruits with mouse or touch gestures.
- Bombs end the game.
- Score system with difficulty scaling.
- Responsive canvas for desktop and mobile.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click **Play**.
3. Move your mouse or swipe to slice fruits.
4. Avoid bombs!
